About ABC Church

ABC Church is a fast-growing congregation in Grand Rapids, Michigan. The church now has approximately 400 registered households and is part of a larger denomination with over 250 churches nationwide. Regional headquarters is in Detroit with other regional offices in New York, Miami, and Seattle.

The church keeps organized records of activities and submits monthly reports on ceremonies and finances to the Detroit office. This system provides a comprehensive web solution for managing these operations.
