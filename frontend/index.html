About Us

MOSA CHURCH AWENDO is a vibrant, inclusive church community centrally located in Sofia, opposite Raila Odinga Stadium, Homabay county, Kenya. Founded on faith, community support, and spiritual growth, our mission is to provide a welcoming home for all to worship, learn, and serve together.

  • Mission: To transform lives through spiritual empowerment, outreach, and fellowship.
  • Vision: Raising a generation of integrity, honor, and positive attraction.
  • Core Values: Integrity, Honor, Attraction, Unity, Service.
