About M.O.S.A (MOUNTAIN OF SOLUTION AND ANSWERS)

M.O.S.A (MOUNTAIN OF SOLUTION AND ANSWERS) is a vibrant, inclusive church community based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. We serve over 400 registered households and are part of a wider fellowship of over 250 churches nationwide. Our regional headquarters is in Detroit, with branches in New York, Miami, and Seattle.

Founded on faith, support, and growth, M.O.S.A strives to provide spiritual guidance, community service, and a welcoming home to all. We organize regular worship services, ceremonies (like baptisms and weddings), educational programs, and outreach events open to everyone.

We are dedicated to transparency and careful record keeping—our monthly activities and finances are reported to our Detroit headquarters.
